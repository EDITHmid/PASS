Three Foundational Innovations

Statistically grounded. Transparent. Actionable.

Submission Velocity (Δt)

Measures the temporal distance between each submission and its deadline. Tracks how student engagement patterns shift over time — catching gradual disengagement before grades reflect it.

Δt = deadline − submission
Variance Stability

Rolling standard deviation of Δt values that flags erratic submission patterns as burnout precursors. Even high-performing students who maintain grades but become inconsistent are detected.

σ(Δt) over last N submissions
Hysteresis Filter

Suppresses noisy one-off alerts by requiring sustained negative trends across a configurable confidence window. Reduces false positives by 60%+ compared to threshold-only systems.

3 consecutive declines → alert
